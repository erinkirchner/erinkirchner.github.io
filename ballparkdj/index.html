What Is This?

It's simple!


Batter Up! is an application that allows you to access a Last.fm playlist containing
the walk-up song(s) of a given MLB player, as well as stats and general player information.


How it works:


  1. Select a team from the first dropdown list.
  2. Select a player from the second dropdown list.
  3. Click the 'Find Player' button.

That's it!
Your selected player's stats, info, and playlist will appear on the right side of the screen.
