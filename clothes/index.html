Consignment Policy

  1. Consignments are accepted by appointment only.
  2. Merchandise must be clean, in good repair, and neatly folded in boxes.
  3. The Clothes Place begins accepting spring and summer items on February 1, fall and winter items August 1.
  4. Unsold merchandise is to be picked up during the last two weeks of February and/or August. Items that are not picked up will be donated to Goodwill.
  5. Store commission is 45% on clothing and used items; 10% on crafts.

The Clothes Place is located in North Bend, Wisconsin, just a quick turn off of state highway 54.
